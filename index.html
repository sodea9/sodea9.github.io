Welcome to my site! This is a collection of my works spread across various disciplines. My broad interests lie at the intersection of art, computing, and mathematics, particularly computer graphics, game engines, optimization, and low-level systems programming.

Featured

I'm currently working on a utility for visualizing and plotting barycentric coordinates. This is useful for learning how trilinear interpolation over a triangle works (useful for texture sampling, interpolating vertex normals, lighting, etc) or for categorizing things as a sum of three categories. Will be released soon after I add a few final features.
