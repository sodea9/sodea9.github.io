Exclusion Zone

2024

narrative short

A young refugee in a near future traverses the barren ruins of UMass Amherst weeks after a local nuclear meltdown. With no service and no transportation, he is left to piece together a crisis that dismantled and evacuated an entire university.

Update: This was recently selected for the 2026 Amherst Wire Film Festival!

Western Massachusetts: Protecting Our Home

2022

essay/documentary

A video essay project concerning the environmental risks present in Western Massachusetts and practices for conservation, considered through the personal lens of a coming-of-age. Additionally features an interview with a CRC River Steward.
